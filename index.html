About

Coding Bee Academy is a specialist coding school for 5 - 18 years old students. We teach computer science, coding and programming skills, and STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) in a fun and accessible environment to your child.

Coding is one of the most important and relevant skills your child can learn today that can translate into a rewarding career. Learning to code heps to strengthen academic performance and allows your child to gain soft-skill like organizational skill and preseverence.

Our prevent lessons are innovative and exciting! We impart life-long skills such as creativity, problem-solving, computational thinking, and teamwork that will help your child success in school and beyond.

Courses

Our program is for students at all skill levels; age 5-18 and process through a broad array of languages from visual prorgamming languages to text-based languages such as Python, Java, Javascript, and more.

Coding Bee Academy's programming language curriculum has helped thousands of students learn about coding. Our lessons are approved by parents and prestigious schools in Indonesia, especially most importantly our curriculum is preferred by our students.
